# 50 个最常见的身体动作单词

# nod /nad/ 点头

Nod if you understand.

/nad if ju ,under'staend/

明白就点头。

# shake /sheik/ 摇

shake your head.

/sheik jer hed/

摇头

# pick /pik/ 挖 / 挑 / 剔牙

  • Don't pick your nose.
    • /dount pik jer nouz/
    • 不要抠鼻子。
  • I use it to pick my teeth.
    • /ai juz it to pik mai tith/
    • 我用它来剔牙。

# blow /blou/ 吹 / 擤

I need to blow my nose.

/ai nid te blou mai nouz/

我要擤鼻涕。

# cough /kaf/ 咳嗽

Cough it up!

/kaf it up/

咳出来！

# sneeze /sniz/ 打喷嚏

Sneeze away from the food.

/sniz e'wei from the fud/

别对着吃的打喷嚏。

# sniff /snif/ 闻 / 嗅

sniff the air

/snif thi er/

闻空气

# plug /plug/ 塞

plug your nose

/plug jer nouz/

塞住你的鼻子

# take a breath /teik a breth/ 喘口气

Take a deep breath.

/teik a dip breth/

深呼吸。

# hold your breath /hould jer breth/ 屏住呼吸

Hold your breath for 5 seconds.

/hould jer breth fer faiv 'sekerndz/

屏住呼吸 5 秒钟。

# open /'oupen/ 打开

open your mouth

/'oupen jer mauth/

张开嘴

# close /klouz/ 关闭

close your eyes

/klouz jer aiz/

闭上眼睛

# stare /ster/ 盯

Stop staring at me.

/stap 'stering at mi/

别盯着我看。

# blink /bligk/ 眨（眼）

Don't blink.

/dount blingk/

不要眨眼。

# hold /hould/ 拿着 / 握着

She's holding a pen.

/shiz 'houlding a pen/

她拿着笔。

# put out /put aut/ 伸出

put out your hand

/put aut jer haend/

伸出你的手

# cross /kros/ 交叉

cross your legs

/kros jer legz/

两腿交叉

# cover /'kuver/ 捂住 / 盖住

cover your face

/’kuver jer feis/

捂脸

# touch /touch/ 触摸

Don't touch it.

/dount touch it/

不要碰它。

# scratch /skreach/ 挠

Try not to scratch.

/trai nat to skreach/

尽量不要挠。

# talk /tok/ 聊天

talk to you later.

/tok to ju ‘Leitar/

我们回聊。

# speak /spik/ 讲话

i don't speak Spanish.

/ai dount soik 'spaenish/

我不讲西班牙语。

# sing /sing/ 唱

sing a song

/sing a song/

唱一首歌

# hum /hum/ 哼（歌）

Hum it for me.

/hum it fer mi/

哼给我听。

# chew /chu/ 咀嚼

Chew it slowly.

/chu it 'slouli/

细嚼慢咽。

# sigh /sai/ 叹

You're sighing a lot.

/jur 'saiing a lot/

你一直叹气。

# clap /klaep/ 拍（手）

clap your hands

/klaep jer heandz/

拍拍手

# slap /slaep/ 扇

she slapped me.

/shi slaept mi/

她扇了我一巴掌。

# pinch /pinch/ 捏 / 掐

Stop pinching me.

/stap 'pinching mi/

别掐我了。

# puch /punch/ 用拳打

Punch him in the head!

/punch im in the head/

锤他的头！

# pull /pul/ 拉

Don't pull so hard.

/dount pul sou hard/

不要用力拉。

# push /push/ 推

Somebody pushed me.

/'sum,bodi pusht mi/

有人推我。

# point /point/ 指

point to your eyes

/point to jer aiz/

指你的眼睛

# shake hands /sheik haendz/ 握手

Let's shake hands.

/lets sheik haendz/

我们握手吧。

# wave /weiv/ 挥手

wave at me

/weiv at mi/

向我挥手

# tickle /'tikel/ 挠痒痒（逗）

Stop tickling me.

/stap 'tikeling mi/

别逗我了。

# rub /rub/ 揉

rub your eyes

/rub jer aiz/

揉眼睛

# squeeze /skwiz/ 挤

squeeze the toothpaste

/skwiz the 'tuth,peist/

挤牙膏

# knock /nak/ 敲

knock on the door

/nak an the dor/

敲门

# grab /graeb/ 抓住

grab his arm

/graeb iz arm/

抓住他的手臂

# hug /hug/ 拥抱

Go hug him.

/gou hug him/

去拥抱他。

# drop /drap/ 掉落

drop the food

/drap the fud/

倒掉食物

# bend /bend/ 弯曲

bend down and pick it up

/bend daun and pik it up/

弯腰捡起来

# crawl /krol/ 爬行

he's crawling in the grass.

/hiz 'kroling in the graes/

他在草丛里爬。

# climb /klaim/ 爬

climb the ladder

/klaim the 'laeder/

爬梯子

# trip /trip/ 绊倒

He tripped over something.

/hi tript 'ouver 'sumthing/

什么东西把他绊倒了。

# slip /slip/ 滑

He slipped on the floor.

/hi slipt an the flor/

他滑倒在地板上。

# kick /kik/ 踢

kick the ball

/kik the bol/

踢那个球。

# jump /jump/ 跳

jump into the water.

/jump 'into the 'woter/

跳进水里。

# run /run/ 跑

I run every day.

/ai run everi dei/

我每天都跑步。

# walk /wok/ 走

walk like this.

/wok laik this/

像这样走。

# crouch /krauch/ 蹲

crouch down a little bit

/krauch daun a 'litel bit/

蹲下去一点

# stretch /strech/ 伸展

stretch your legs

/strech jer legz/

拉伸双腿
